Privacy Policy

Privacy Summary

Tasbih Counter is designed with your privacy in mind. The app operates completely offline and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. No internet connection is required.

Information We Collect

Tasbih Counter does not collect any personal information from its users. The app:

  • Does not require internet access
  • Does not collect personal data
  • Does not track user behavior
  • Does not use analytics services
  • Does not contain advertisements
  • Does not require user registration or login

Data Storage

All data created within the Tasbih Counter app (such as your count numbers and settings) is stored locally on your device only. This information:

  • Remains on your device at all times
  • Is not transmitted to any external servers
  • Is not accessible to us or any third parties
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the app

Permissions

Tasbih Counter does not require any special permissions on your device. The app operates with minimal system access and does not request:

  • Internet access
  • Camera or microphone access
  • Location services
  • Contact information
  • Storage access beyond app data

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks. It functions as a completely standalone application.

Children's Privacy

Tasbih Counter is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. Since I do not collect any personal information, there are no privacy concerns regarding children's use of the app.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Since the app does not collect data, changes will typically be minimal and related to clarifications or legal requirements.
